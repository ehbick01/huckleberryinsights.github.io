Who I Am

Hello! My name is Eric Bickel - and independent consultant looking to use data to help clients solve problems. Most of this site houses personal analysis and write-ups on interesting tools and datasets that I’ve come across while nerding out - but I hope that as a portfolio, they are interesting and fun to read while also showcasing some of what I can help clients out with!

What I Offer

As an analyst, I have over ten years of experience working with data to build new tools and drive new insights. In my day job, I manage a team of data scientists within a large spirits manufacturer - linking data to insight for business customers. I bring a wealth of knowledge of statistical modeling and business acumen, and tons of experience of all the buzzwords in between.

Most importantly, I’ve been doing this long enough that I know the difference between what can be done and what should be done. Armed with this, I can build strategic and operational tools driven by data and armed with research to help make strong decisions.
